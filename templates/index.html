Lab 16 instructions

How this works

1
You upload an image
The form sends your file to Flask's /upload route.
2
Flask uploads it to S3
boto3.upload_fileobj() puts the file in your [initials]-image-source bucket.
3
S3 fires an event — Lambda wakes up automatically
The moment the file lands in S3, AWS calls your Lambda function. No polling, no cron job.
4
Lambda flips the image
Pillow flips it upside down and saves the result to your [initials]-image-source-processed bucket.
5
Flask shows you the result
Pre-signed S3 URLs are generated for both the original and flipped image and displayed below.
